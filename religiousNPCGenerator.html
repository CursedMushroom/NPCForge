What everything does:

Amount of NPCs to generate: Determines the number of NPCs to be created in one go. Each time you generate new NPCs, the current pool will reset unless individual NPCs are locked.

Hierarchy Position Ratios: Influences the socioeconomic status of the generated NPCs, impacting their careers and clothing. The ratios will define how many NPCs hold high, mid, or low-ranking positions within their religious or church hierarchy, affecting their influence and power within the organization.

Races: Currently offers a comprehensive selection of nearly all races available in Dungeons & Dragons. Select from various races to determine which types of characters will be generated, influencing their appearance, abilities, and cultural background.

Presets: Provides predefined settings for generating NPCs based on common themes or scenarios. Choose from options like Traditional Hierarchies, Fantasy Orders, Divine Hierarchies, Arcane Orders, or Mystic Cults to tailor the generated NPCs to specific types of religious or church organizations.

Reset All: Resets all settings to their defaults. Wealth distribution will be set to random/any, races will be cleared, and presets will be deselected, giving you a fresh start for your next batch of NPCs.

Gender: Specifies the gender of the generated NPCs. Options include Male, Female, Androgynous, or Any, allowing for a diverse range of characters suitable for various narrative needs.

Clothing: Select the style of clothing that NPCs will be wearing, including options such as Luxurious, Practical, Simple, or Mystical. This choice will reflect their socioeconomic status and role within the religious hierarchy.

Breakdown Card: Provides detailed information about each generated NPC, including:

  • Religious Symbol: Displays the emblem or icon associated with the NPC's religious order or deity. This symbol represents their faith and role within their religious community.
  • Alignment: Indicates the moral and ethical stance of the NPC, such as Lawful Good, Neutral Evil, etc. Alignment helps to determine their motivations and behavior.
  • Colors: Lists the colors associated with the NPC's religious order or personal preferences. These colors often symbolize their faith and can be seen in their clothing, symbols, and other personal items.
  • Story Hook: Offers a potential plot point or story element related to the NPC. This could include their role in ongoing events, a secret they guard, or a quest they are involved in.

| : This will lock the NPC, allowing it to stay on refresh. Unlocking it will allow it to leave on refresh.

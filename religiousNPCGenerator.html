Religious Npc's Generator

Welcome to the Religious NPCs Generator! This tool is designed to help you create rich, detailed, and diverse religious characters for your role-playing games, storytelling, or creative projects. This willgenerate a group of NPC' that belong to the same religous group, with the option to generate a "Breakdown card", which will layout more info on the religon the genreated group follows.

What everything does:

(Hide info)

Amount of NPCs to generate: Determines the number of NPCs to be created in one go. Each time you generate new NPCs, the current pool will reset unless individual NPCs are locked.

Hierarchy Position Ratios: Influences the socioeconomic status of the generated NPCs, impacting their careers and clothing. The ratios will define how many NPCs hold high, mid, or low-ranking positions within their religious or church hierarchy, affecting their influence and power within the organization.

Races: Currently offers a comprehensive selection of nearly all races available in Dungeons & Dragons. Select from various races to determine which types of characters will be generated, influencing their appearance, abilities, and cultural background.

Presets: Provides predefined settings for generating NPCs based on common themes or scenarios. Choose from options like Traditional Hierarchies, Fantasy Orders, Divine Hierarchies, Arcane Orders, or Mystic Cults to tailor the generated NPCs to specific types of religious or church organizations. These presets pull from a more specific pool of roles, clothing, and will have more taliored breakdown to fit the theme.

Reset All: Resets all settings to their defaults. Wealth distribution will be set to random/any, races will be cleared, and presets will be deselected, giving you a fresh start for your next batch of NPCs.

Gender: Specifies the gender of the generated NPCs. Options include Male, Female, and Non-Binary/Androgynous, allowing for a diverse range of characters suitable for various narrative needs.

Clothing: Select the style of clothing that NPCs will be wearing, including options such as Luxurious, Practical, Simple, or Mystical. This choice will reflect their socioeconomic status and role within the religious hierarchy.

Breakdown Card (the dark grey card): Provides detailed information about the religion the generated NPC's follow:

  • Religious Symbol: Provides the emblem or icon associated with the their religious order or deity.
  • Alignment: Indicates the moral and ethical stance of the religous group, such as Lawful Good, Neutral Evil, etc. Alignment helps to determine their motivations and behavior.
  • Hierarchy Position Ratios: Influences the socioeconomic status of the generated NPCs, impacting their careers and clothing. The ratios will define how many NPCs hold high, mid, or low-ranking positions within their religious or church hierarchy, affecting their influence and power within the organization.
  • Prompt: Offers a potential plot point or story element related to the religious group. This could include their role in ongoing events, a secret they guard, or a quest they are involved in.
  • Colors: Lists the colors associated with the religious order. These colors often symbolize their faith and can be seen in their clothing, symbols, and other personal items on the NPC's.

| : This will lock the NPC, allowing it to stay on refresh. Unlocking it will allow it to leave on refresh. If the breakdown card is locked, a new one will NOT be generated.

: This will delete the NPC from the current NPC pool.

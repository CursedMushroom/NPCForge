Town NPC's Generator

Welcome to the Town NPC's Generator, a tool for creating diverse and dynamic non-player characters (NPCs) for tabletop RPG campaigns or fantasy stories. This generator provides a wide range of NPC details. Appearance , personality traits and relationships to name a few. Whether you need a handful of characters to populate a bustling town or specific individuals to enrich your narrative, hopfully each NPC can help bring your world to life.

What everything does:

Amount of Npc's to generate: Determines the number of NPCs to be created in one go. Each time you generate new NPCs, the current pool will reset unless individual NPCs are locked.

Wealth Distribution: Influences the socioeconomic status of the generated NPCs, impacting their careers and clothing.

Races: Currently Offers a comprehensive selection of nearly all races available in Dungeons & Dragons.

Presets: Provides pre-configured combinations of races and wealth distributions based on popular settings from D&D books. These presets help quickly populate your town with themed NPCs.

Reset All: Resets all settings to their defaults. Wealth distribution will be set to random/any, races will be cleared, and presets will be deselected, giving you a fresh start for your next batch of NPCs.

| : This will lock the NPC, allowing it to stay on a refreash, unlocking it will allow it to leave on refreash.

: This will delete the NPC from the current NPC pool.
